About

I'm a PhD researcher at CentraleSupélec (LGI, Université Paris-Saclay), working with GE HealthCare on AI-driven digital twins for the circular supply chain of MRI and CT spare parts. The core question: when a part disappears from a global system, did it physically leave, or did the data just lose track of it?

My toolkit sits at the intersection of reinforcement learning, graph machine learning, and probabilistic modeling, applied to messy, large-scale industrial data. On the side, I care a lot about language models that work natively in Arabic and other underserved languages, which is where my NLP projects live.

Before the PhD: a double engineering degree from École Centrale Casablanca and CentraleSupélec (AI specialization), with stops at SLB, Carrefour, and Illuin Technology along the way.
